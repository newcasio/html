Services

Our experienced Veterinary staff provide a full range of clinical, diagnostic and surgical services in our new purpose built premises.

Our Hospital

We have a brand new hospital building with full surgery facilities and experienced surgeons for both routine and complex surgeries. Both dog and cat wards are individually temperature controlled.

We provide routine consultations, xray/diagnostic imaging, vaccinations, dog and cat de-sexing and dental care/dental therapy. Our experienced surgeons perform both routine and complex surgeries.

In association with Adelaide Mobile Vet we also provide a comprehensive home visit service that services all suburbs of Adelaide. We provide the majority of our own after hours service.

We also carry an extensive range of pet health care products, including a full range of flea and worming products, Hill’s Prescription Diet food and Rogz collars, leads, and accessories.

Full Diagnostic Facilities

Advances in veterinary medicine have given us many new tools to help make accurate diagnosis of illnesses in animals. At Playford Vet we utilize:

  • Xray
  • Ultrasound
  • ECG and
  • Full Laboratory Facilities

as part of our diagnostic procedures.

To ensure the best results we use the services of a full time veterinary ultrasound specialist at our clinic.

Our laboratory testing is performed by IDEXX Laboratories, where we generally receive results either same day or overnight. This gives us the highest degree of accuracy, a much wider range of testing options and allows for interpretation by specialist veterinary pathologists.

Comprehensive Range of Surgical Procedures

At Playford Vet we pride ourselves on the range and quality of surgical procedures that we offer.

Our senior surgeon has over 30 years’ experience with post-graduate training in orthopaedic and soft tissue surgery.

In almost all cases we use buried dissolving stitches to close the skin. This means no stitches are visible and your pet won’t need to wear a plastic cone to prevent them from licking at their wound.

Pain relief is routinely administered before and after surgery. We endeavour to make your pet’s recovery as smooth and pain free as possible.

Latest Anaesthetic Monitoring

We understand the anxiety many owners feel when their pet needs to undergo an anaesthetic.

The anaesthetic protocols we use at the Playford Vet are amongst the safest drug combinations available.

All animals have respiratory, pulse and blood oxygen monitoring throughout their anaesthetic.

IV fluids are administered routinely during anaesthetics at no additional charge.

Climate Controlled Wards

Our purpose built clinic includes separate dog and cat wards that are maintained at an optimum temperature through their own climate control systems.

The spacious runs cater for even the largest breed of dog.
