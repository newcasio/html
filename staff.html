Our staff

Dr Brett Boling

Our principal vet Dr Brett Boling has over 30 years’ experience and has completed post-graduate training in orthopaedic surgery, soft tissue surgery, cardiac and respiratory medicine, advanced diagnostic and clinical therapeutics. He also has had more than 10 years’ experience in emergency medicine prior to starting Adelaide Mobile Vet.

Ms Manyana Starkie JP (Yana)

Our business manager Ms Manyana (Yana) Starkie JP Adv Dip MST, Dip RM, RT, Cert PMP (Adel University) has been involved with our veterinary businesses since 2000. Not only does she keep our business on track in all areas, she also has extensive experience at helping our clients through often difficult times with their pets' illnesses. Please feel free to call her regarding the services our clinic provides.

Tommy

Tommy, our Customer Relations Officer, is our meet and greet officer at the clinic and has been with us from 8 weeks of age. He has 12 years’ experience in the role and specializes in appearing with one of his extensive range of soft toys upon your arrival. His wagging tail has calmed the nerves of many an anxious patient.

Diana Lam

Diana has been with us for a couple of years now. You will often be greeted with her cheerful smile in the mornings. Diana's speciality is making sure that your pets' stay with us is a comfortable one. If you've ever wondered why your pet smells so wonderful when you collect them, it's because Diana attends to their personal needs. Diana works between the Gardens Vet and Playford Vet Clinics.

Sherice Milham

Cert IV Vet Nurse

Sherice is a qualified Cert IV Vet Nurse. She has had work experience with many clinics in Adelaide and thoroughly enjoyed all aspects of veterinary nursing. Sherice is particularly interested in emergency nursing. You will see Sherice working at both our Gardens Vet and Playford Vet Clinics.

Rebecca Maguire

Cert IV Vet Nurse

Rebecca is a qualified Cert IV Vet Nurse and works part-time at our clinics. She brings with her several years of vet nursing experience. Rebecca has an interest in orthopaedic and emergency vet nursing.

You will see Rebecca at both our Gardens Vet and Playford Vet Clinics where she will be more than happy to assist with enquiries and appointments.

Sophie Wilson

Cert III in Companion Animal Services

Sophie has recently joined our team in a part-time role whilst still completing her Cert IV Vet Nursing qualifications. She comes with many years of customer service experience.

Whilst studying at TAFE, Sophie has gained invaluable experience in our industry and looks forward to excelling as a Vet Nurse. You will see Sophie working at both our Gardens Vet and Playford Vet Clinics.
